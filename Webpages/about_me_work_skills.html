Work Experience

Swee Heng Balery Pte Ltd. (Sales Assistant)

I worked in Swee Heng Bakery in for 5 months as a part time sales assistant. As a sales assistant, I have to learn how to provide customers with fair, friendly and courteous service. Opening and closing the store together with counting the cash balance in the cash drawer was also part of the job scope of a sales assistant.

Through this job, I learn how to manage customers' demands and understand the importance of delivering quality service to the customers. I also developed resilience and was more quick witted due the fast paced environment.

Yamato Transport (Delivery Assistant)

As a Delivery Assistant, I was tasked with following the driver around Singapore Island delivering items such as parcels, water tubs and documents. I was also taught how to manage documents, uncontactable customers as well as dissatisfied customers. Overall providing customers with a pleasant delivery experience was the most vital role as a Delivery Assistant.

I was able to gain a better insight as to how the logistics industry function and empathise with delivery workers who are trying their best to provide their service for customers during this Covid-19 Pandemic.

Temperature Screener (North Point City)

The role of a temperature screener was relatively simple, check that people entering the mall have scanned the SafeEntry QR Code or tap their phones/tokens on the Bluetooth device.

However it was not all that simple as there were members of public who were unwilling to cooperate and abide by this Covid-19 measure. There also people who would try to sneak pass temperature screeners like myself to get into the mall without checking in. As a staff I need to stop them from doing so as this would pose a danger for everyone else in the mall.
